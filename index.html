Restaurant of the Week


Near and Far Camden

48 Chalk Farm Road, London NW1 8AJ

The concept is inspired by world travels, import and export, and particularly Palm Springs, with its desert-meets-oasis aesthetic. Expect lush greens, palm trees, gorgeous wallpaper, neon signs and desert shades.

Their venues serve quirky signature cocktails, fine wines, top notch coffee (Angel and Camden only) and a great selection of non-alcoholic drinks, plus delicious food options at all sites. They set the scene for a laidback party environment with DJs playing everything from Latin house and disco, to reggae and Afrobeat.

Take a trip to a Near & Far, and let the world come to you, through drinks, food and music.
